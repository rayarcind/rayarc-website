Our Mission

Aerospace Education & Outreach

We are working towards delivering educational workshops, bootcamps, and interactive learning programs for schools and universities. Through our in-house simulation tools and hands-on demonstrations, we aim to make rocketry and aerospace concepts more accessible, affordable, and engaging, fostering scientific temperament and innovation among students across India.

Software Development

We are developing in-house simulation, telemetry, and data analysis software to support aerospace research and testing. These tools are designed to predict motor performance, analyze static and dynamic test data, and assist future mission operations. They also serve as powerful educational platforms for learning and engineering validation.
