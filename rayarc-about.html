Our Vision

India's aerospace potential, unlocked.

India has immense scientific and engineering potential, yet opportunities for structured amateur rocketry and practical aerospace development remain limited. RayArc was founded with the belief that innovation begins when curiosity meets experimentation.

Our vision is to help cultivate a stronger aerospace ecosystem where students, researchers, and innovators can actively participate in engineering — rather than simply study it.

Active Research Ongoing
Why We Exist

A culture of experimental engineering.

Throughout the world, amateur rocketry has served as a gateway for future scientists, engineers, and aerospace pioneers. We believe India deserves a similarly strong culture of experimental engineering and aerospace exploration.

RayArc aims to contribute to that future by encouraging hands-on learning, independent research, and technical development. We strive to create an environment where ideas can be tested, data can be gathered, and engineering concepts can evolve beyond theory into real-world applications.

From Ideas to Data

Experimentation
as a methodology.

At RayArc, we believe that meaningful technological progress is built upon experimentation and evidence. Whether investigating propulsion characteristics, analyzing grain geometries, developing simulation tools, or validating engineering models — our approach is centered around learning through testing and observation.

Every experiment, simulation, and analysis contributes to a growing body of knowledge that guides future development.

Our Journey

Looking Forward

We view today's research as the foundation for tomorrow's missions. As our capabilities expand, we aim to undertake increasingly advanced aerospace projects, integrating the knowledge gained through years of experimentation, engineering development, and technical research. The long-term objective is to transform ideas into capable systems, innovative technologies, and ambitious aerospace missions that push the boundaries of what can be achieved.
